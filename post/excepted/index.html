Storage Third Party Data

Many people heard a joke about how to put an elephant in fridge.

The steps are clear and simple.But how to store Third Party data in database?

Can you come up with a method that is as simple as the joke said.

I am not,so i often can easily find out where are bugs in my program.

my job is that relay user data to Third Party and store the response.

Recently i have some experience and want to share to you:

  1. Verify the received data.

If received data not illegal,program should not request Third Party.

  1. Request Third Party.

    1. transform recived data to satisfy the requirement posted by Third Party.

    2. send request.

  2. Parse the response.

    • The response of Third Party is not always one style.
    • Even though the style is right,but you may not need to store all data.
    • saved in database needs some style.

  3. Storage.

    • you should be careful of this step.Although the data has been parsed,it may still have some bugs.So the “try… except…” is necessary.

When i finally figured out the four steps,i suddenly recalled a method of organize struct:“top-down”.

The premise of top-down is that building a clear module.
